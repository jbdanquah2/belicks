ADMISSION NOTICE


BELICKS is a private community school with the mission to “provide a safe and nurturing environment where all children can learn to achieve success” hence the motto: descendo Floremus meaning through learning we flourish. The school currently run from Creche through to Class 1 and intend to run through to JHS.

OUR FACILITIES:

  • Small class size – maximum of 15 - 20 children in a class
  • Combination of Montessori and GES method of teaching
  • Conducive classrooms that facilitate effective teaching and learning process
  • Well groomed teachers and attendants

DEPARTMENTS:

  • Creche – 1 to 2 years
  • Nursery 1 – 2 to 3 years
  • Nursery 2 – 3 to 4 years
  • KG 1 – 4 to 5 years
  • KG 2 – 5 to 6 years
  • Class 1 – 6 years
